## Design Context

### Users

Spark Alliance serves university students who move between study, planning, campus social life, health, shopping, collaboration, and career growth throughout the day. The product should let them understand today's state quickly, take the next action with minimal friction, and move between modules without losing context.

### Brand Personality

Premium, youthful, and purposeful. The interface should feel like a coherent campus universe: optimistic and imaginative, but still calm enough for repeated daily use. Chinese cultural motifs may appear as refined details rather than ornamental clutter.

### Aesthetic Direction

The canonical visual source is the existing artwork under `DesingImage/` and the matching blueprints/tokens under `tasks/`. Recreate those references as faithfully as practical at their target viewport sizes. The shared language is a deep cosmic dark theme with near-black violet surfaces, restrained glass, crisp indigo/purple light, small gold and functional-color accents, custom luminous illustration assets, compact information density, and clear operational hierarchy.

Generic design-system recommendations are advisory only. When they conflict with a reference screenshot, module README, blueprint, or token file, the project reference wins. In particular, do not replace the reference palette with rose social-app colors, do not turn operational pages into marketing layouts, and do not add decorative gradients or glass effects that are absent from the source.

### Design Principles

1. **Reference before invention.** Match composition, density, proportion, color, typography, and interaction states from the supplied design images before adding interpretation.
2. **One universe, distinct modules.** Preserve the global shell and cosmic material system while retaining each module's own accent colors and domain-specific imagery.
3. **Operational clarity.** Dense pages must remain scannable: stable grids, restrained radii, aligned numerics, predictable navigation, and one obvious primary action per region.
4. **Real interactions stay real.** Preserve current routes, Supabase-backed behavior, local-first state, loading/error/empty states, and keyboard semantics while changing presentation.
5. **Responsive by recomposition.** Desktop, tablet, and mobile layouts should deliberately reflow rather than merely shrink. Critical actions remain reachable with 44px minimum targets and no horizontal overflow.
6. **Evidence over impression.** Every visual wave is checked against the canonical screenshots at matching viewports. A wave is not accepted until `visual-verdict` scores it at least 90 and functional verification passes.
